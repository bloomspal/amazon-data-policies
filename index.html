Privacy and Data Handling Policies

At BloomsPal, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of data collected from Amazon. Below, we outline our practices for collecting, processing, storing, using, sharing, and disposing of this data.

Data Collection

We collect the following information from Amazon:

  • Orders in "unshipped" status from your store.
  • Order items included in each order.
  • Destination address for the shipment.
  • Shipping status updates, including tracking numbers for each package.

Details about how this data is accessed can be found in the Amazon Seller Partner API documentation:

  • Orders filtered by “unshipped” status: GetOrders
  • Order items within each order: GetOrderItems
  • Shipping address: GetOrderAddress
  • Shipping information updates (tracking numbers and packages): GetOrderShippingInfo

Data Processing

The data collected is processed strictly to facilitate the preparation and shipment of orders.

Purpose:

  • Ensuring accurate picking and packing of products.
  • Coordinating timely and correct delivery to the customer’s address.

Data Storage

  • All collected data is stored securely in our database.
  • Data is retained until delivery is completed, plus an additional 60 days for record-keeping and auditing purposes.
  • After the 60-day retention period, the data is permanently and securely deleted from our systems.

Data Usage

  • The collected data is used exclusively to manage orders, prepare shipments, and track delivery progress.
  • Only the destination address is shared with our carrier partner, FedEx, to ensure accurate delivery.
  • We do not use the data for marketing, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to order fulfillment.

Data Sharing

We share data only when necessary to fulfill customer orders:

  • Carrier Partners: The destination address is provided to facilitate shipment.
  • No other third parties receive or have access to the collected Amazon data.

Data Disposal

  • Once the retention period of 60 days post-delivery ends, all data is securely deleted from our database.
  • We employ industry-standard methods to ensure the safe and irreversible disposal of data.

For any questions or concerns regarding our privacy and data handling practices, please contact wecare@bloomspal.com.
